Streaming Help
How to Stream with OBS Studio

After creating your stream, you'll get a unique stream key. Configure OBS as follows:

  1. Go to Settings > Stream
  2. Select "Custom..." as the service
  3. Enter the server URL: rtmp://{{ request.host }}
  4. Enter your stream key
  5. Click "Start Streaming"
