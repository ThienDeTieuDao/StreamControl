Getting Started

Creating a live stream is easy!

  1. Click the "Create Your First Stream" button
  2. Fill in your stream details and settings
  3. Get your stream key and configure your software
  4. Start streaming from your broadcasting software
Stream Ideas

Not sure what to stream? Here are some ideas:

  • Gaming sessions
  • Coding and development
  • Live podcasts or discussions
  • Tutorials and educational content
  • Q&A sessions
  • Art and creative works
Streaming Help

Need help getting started?

  • Make sure you have streaming software installed (OBS, StreamLabs, etc.)
  • Use RTMP protocol with your stream key
  • Set your resolution to 720p (1280x720) for best results
  • Use a bitrate between 2500-4000 Kbps
  • For help configuring OBS, check our guide
